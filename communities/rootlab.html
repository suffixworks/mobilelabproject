Root Lab

Root Lab is a genetic experiment about social space. We are interested in visual and sound media based on contexts of society, politics and culture, focusing on works from newcomers who are still studying or fresh graduates. We hope this visual and sound art showcase will be a space where artists of the same age and interest can meet up and exchange points of view in order to develop their works later on.
