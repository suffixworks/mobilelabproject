Gaze & Hear

HD · 10 min · Stereo · Colour · 2011

A video-art experiment and survey on human visual and sound perception and their influence on way of life, national integration, and belief in fact. The video changes usual perception by pairing a Thai ancient tale, read in a calm voice, with annoying visuals and sound.

Director — Nontawat Numbenchapol Script Writer — Donsaron Voice-over & Sound Recorder — Aimsinthu Ramasoot, Gunn Karalak Sound Design — Katenan Juntimathorn Moving Image & Poster Design — Jate Saitthiti
