Ratchapruek

1 min 30 sec · Video Installation · HD · Colour · No dialogue · 2012

Ratchapruek (Cassia fistula), known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Asia. It is the national tree of Thailand, and its flower is Thailand’s national flower.
