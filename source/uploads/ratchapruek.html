Ratchapruek
1.30 minutes, Video Installation, HD, Colour, No Dialog, 2012

Ratchapruek (Cassia fistula), known as the golden shower tree and other names, is a flowering plant in the family Fabaceae, native to southern Asia, from southern Pakistan east through India to Myanmar and south to Sri Lanka. It is associated with the Mullai region of Sangam landscape. It is the national tree of Thailand, and its flower is Thailand's national flower.
